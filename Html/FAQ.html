FAQ

Question Answer
How can I update a swapped book? To update a swap, simply visit the 'Swap History' page and click on 'Add Swap'.
Is swapping books free? Yes, all book swaps are free of charge. However, shipping is the swapper's responsibility.
How do I search for a book? Use the search bar on the Home page to find any book you are interested in.
Can I add books to my wish list? Absolutely! All you have to do is click 'Add book to Wishlist' in your Wishlist. You can see it in your profile.
How do I start a chat with another user? Go to the 'Chats' page, select the user you wish to chat with, and start typing your message.
